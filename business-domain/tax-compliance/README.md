Enterprise tax compliance and reporting.

## Features

- **VAT/GST Management**: Tax calculation, filing, reclaim
- **Sales Tax**: Nexus determination, rate calculation, returns
- **1099 Reporting**: Vendor classification, form generation
- **Tax Provision**: Income tax calculation, deferred tax
- **Compliance Analytics**: Tax rates, liability tracking, audit preparation

## Architecture

**Layer**: 2 (Domain Services)\
**Dependencies**: canon, database, accounting, sales, payables, drizzle-orm, zod
